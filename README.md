# Baseball Statistics Analysis

> A comprehensive statistical analysis project developed with **R Markdown**, the first project focusing on the MLB performance prediction of pitchers from KBO, and the second project focusing on the effectiveness of stealing bases. The insights were officially published as a web journal.

> **[Link for Performance Prediciton project](https://www.bruinsportsanalytics.com/post/erick-fedde-woo-suk-go)**\
> **[Link for Analysis of Stealing Bases project](https://www.bruinsportsanalytics.com/post/stealing-bases)**

## 1. Project Overview
*   **Duration**: January, 2024 – May, 2024
*   **Outcome**: Published a data-driven report (Journal) on Bruin Sports Analytics website.
*   **Goal**: To predict performance in MLB of pitchers from KBO, analyze the effectiveness and contribution to winning percentage of stealing bases, and communicate complex statistical insights through a professional and accessible journal format.

## 2. Tech Stack & Environment
*   **Language**: R
*   **Format**: R Markdown (.Rmd) — *Integrated code, analysis, and visualization.*
*   **Tools**: RStudio, Excel (Data Management)

## 3. Key Insights & Impact
  * Created estimated pitching statistics of Woo-Suk Go and Erick Fedde
  * Identified that an increase in the number of stolen bases and its success rate was not effective enough to result in a dramatic increase in the winning percentage of MLB teams from 2022 to 2023.
